About Phizze

Phizze.com is a free educational platform that provides interactive simulations, visualizations, and a multitude of physics, mathamtics, and engineering resources.

The idea started when I was learning about mathmatics and physics during my collage study, where I would spend hours searching and reading about different topics through some pretty awesome websites, but still I wished if there were a platform where I can visualize and interact with the concepts I was learning at the time! a place where I can see the effects of moment of inertia on a bending beam, or the relation between velocity and pressure in a closed pipe! and so through that and in the hope of creating something that might help others, Phizze was born.
